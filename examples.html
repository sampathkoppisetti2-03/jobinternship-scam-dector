REAL-WORLD ANALYSIS

Fake vs Genuine

Side-by-side forensic analysis of real scam communications vs legitimate job offers. Train your eye to spot the differences.

01
Email-Based Job Offer — "TCS Recruitment"
🚨 Scam / Fake Offer
Email Client
From: tcs-hr-recruitment@gmail.com ⚠️ FAKE
Subj: YOU ARE SELECTED!! TCS Job Offer - ₹35,000/Month - IMMEDIATE JOINING
To: Undisclosed Recipients
Dear Candidate,

Congratulations!! You have been SELECTED for a Software Developer position at TCS (Tata Consultancy Services) with a salary of ₹35,000/month + incentives.

NO INTERVIEW REQUIRED. This is a DIRECT HIRING drive.

To CONFIRM YOUR SEAT, please pay a refundable security deposit of ₹2,500 via UPI: tcs.hiring@paytm

⚡ HURRY! Last 3 seats. Pay within 2 hours to confirm.

Regards,
HR Team, TCS Recruitment Cell

🔍 Red Flags Found

🚨
@gmail.com — Real TCS uses @tcs.com exclusively
🚨
Legitimate companies never skip the interview process entirely
🚨
₹2,500 "security deposit" demanded via UPI — classic scam
🚨
"2 hours" deadline to prevent verification
🚨
"Undisclosed Recipients" — mass spam email
✅ Legitimate Offer
Email Client
From: careers.noreply@tcs.com ✓ VERIFIED
Subj: Your TCS NQT Application — Interview Scheduled
To: rahul.sharma@email.com (your exact name)
Dear Rahul Sharma,

Thank you for applying to TCS National Qualifier Test (NQT 2024). We are pleased to invite you for a technical interview for the role of Systems Engineer.

Interview Details:
Date: 15 January 2024, 10:00 AM IST
Mode: Virtual (Microsoft Teams)
Recruiter: Priya Nair | priya.nair@tcs.com

No payment is required at any stage of TCS recruitment. If you are asked for money, report to: anti-scam@tcs.com

Regards,
TCS Talent Acquisition Team
CIN: L22210MH1995PLC084781

✅ Legitimacy Markers

✅
@tcs.com — matches company website exactly
✅
Proper interview process with date, time, and platform details
✅
Explicitly states no payment required and provides fraud reporting email
✅
Addressed to your exact full name — not "Dear Candidate"
✅
Company registration number included — verifiable on mca.gov.in

WhatsApp from unknown number — no official communication
🚨
You never applied — how were you "selected"?

₹999 registration fee via UPI to unknown ID

Official internship platform with verified companies
✅

03
Offer Letter Document Analysis
🚨 Fake Offer Letter Signs
🚨
Wrong logo, different font from official website, no registered address
🚨
Missing or fake CIN number — cannot be verified on MCA portal
🚨
₹40,000–₹60,000 for fresher with zero experience — unrealistic
🚨
"Pay ₹3,000 security deposit" before joining date
🚨
Spelling errors, inconsistent formatting, wrong city names
🚨
No name or designation of the signing authority — just "HR Team"
✅ Genuine Offer Letter Signs
✅
Official letterhead matching website exactly, with GSTIN/CIN
✅
Valid CIN number verifiable on mca.gov.in within seconds
✅
Market-realistic CTC with clear breakup (basic, HRA, PF, bonus)
✅
No payment required — only original documents for BGC
✅
Proofread, consistent, professional language throughout
✅
Named signatory with designation, employee ID, and contact email
