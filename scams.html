Know Your Enemy

Every job scam follows a pattern. Understanding these 12 documented scam types is your first line of defense.

💸
Payment

Registration / Training Fee Scam

Scammers offer jobs then demand upfront "registration fees", "training material charges", or "ID card fees" ranging from ₹500–₹5,000. Payment is made via UPI and communication stops.

Risk
95%
🎣
Phishing

Fake Application Portal

A cloned website mimicking Naukri, LinkedIn, or a real company tricks victims into submitting resumes with Aadhaar, PAN, bank details — used for identity theft or financial fraud.

Risk
90%
👤
Fake HR

Impersonation of Real Companies

Fraudsters impersonate HR departments of TCS, Wipro, Infosys, Amazon, or Google using near-identical email domains and fabricated offer letters to extract personal data or money.

Risk
88%
📦
Payment

Work-From-Home Product Scam

Victims are hired for WFH packing/data-entry jobs and asked to buy a "starter kit" of ₹2,000–₹10,000. After payment, contact ceases and no work is provided.

Risk
85%
📧
Phishing

Fake Interview Invite Phishing

Email/WhatsApp messages send a "scheduled interview" link leading to malware downloads or credential-harvesting pages disguised as Zoom/Google Meet join pages.

Risk
82%
🪪
Identity

KYC & Document Harvesting

"Complete KYC to receive your offer letter" — victims submit Aadhaar, PAN, selfies, and bank statements. These are used for loan fraud, SIM swapping, and financial crimes.

Risk
92%
🔒
Payment

Security Deposit Scam

After receiving a fake offer letter, candidates are asked to pay a "refundable security deposit" of ₹5,000–₹50,000 to "confirm their slot." The money is never returned.

Risk
97%
💬
Fake HR

WhatsApp / Telegram HR Scam

Unsolicited WhatsApp messages from "HR consultants" offering high-paying remote jobs. Interviews happen over chat. Victims are hired instantly, then asked to complete tasks that require payment.

Risk
75%
🏦
Identity

Bank Account Mule Recruitment

Victims are "hired" as "payment processors" and asked to receive money in their accounts and forward it — unknowingly becoming money mules in laundering operations, leading to legal trouble.

Risk
99%
🤖
Phishing

AI-Generated Fake Job Postings

Using GPT-generated descriptions and stolen company logos, scammers create ultra-convincing job postings on legitimate platforms like Indeed and LinkedIn with fake company profiles.

Risk
70%
📋
Fake HR

Fake Internship Certificate Scam

Students are offered free internships with "guaranteed certificates." After completing real work (content writing, data entry) for weeks, the certificate is withheld unless a fee is paid.

Risk
65%
🎰
Payment

Task-Based Investment Scam

"Complete tasks like liking YouTube videos and earn ₹500 per task!" Victims earn small amounts initially, then are asked to invest increasing amounts to unlock "bigger earnings" — a classic Ponzi trap.

Risk
93%
